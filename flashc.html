Smart Energy Hubs in India

Puga Geothermal Energy Project, Ladakh
India's first geothermal power plant, located in Puga Valley, Ladakh. Operations by ONGC.
Bhadla Solar Park, Rajasthan
One of the world's largest solar parks with a capacity of 2.25 GW, a joint effort by RSPDCL, Saurya Urja, and Adani.
Jaisalmer Wind Park, Rajasthan
India's second-largest onshore wind project, developed by Suzlon Energy with a budget of Rs 350.05 crore.
Rewa Solar Project, Madhya Pradesh
A 750 MW capacity solar project developed by Rewa Ultra Mega Solar Limited. Supplies power to Indian Railways.
Brahmanvel Wind Farm, Maharashtra
Wind farm with a capacity of 528 MW, developed by Parakh Agro Industries, located in Dhule.
Gobar-Dhan Bio-CNG Plant, Indore
Asia's largest Bio-CNG plant, producing 17,000 kg CNG daily, inaugurated by PM Modi.
Vankusawade Wind Park, Maharashtra
570.15 MW capacity, developed by Suzlon Energy, located near Satara.
Ramky Enviro Landfill Gas to Bio-CNG Plant, Hyderabad
World's first landfill gas to compressed biogas plant with a capacity of 755 Nm³/hr, developed by Ramky Enviro Engineers Limited.
Shakti Sthala Solar Project, Karnataka
World's largest solar park, with a capacity of 2,000 MW, spanning 13,000 acres.
